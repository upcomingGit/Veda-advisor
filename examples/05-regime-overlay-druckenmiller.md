# Example 05 - Druckenmiller: Cut or Hold in a Regime Shift

## User question

My position is down 30%. Should I average down or cut it?

## What Druckenmiller checks

1. Has the macro regime changed against this trade?
2. Is the original thesis broken on new facts?
3. Is there a clear kill criterion?

## Decision

Cut.

The thesis is broken on current data. First loss is better than delayed loss.

## Would be wrong if

1. The thesis is still intact and this is only a temporary regime drawdown.
2. A predefined kill criterion was not actually triggered.
