# Example 06 - Marks: Volatility or Permanent Loss

## User question

The stock dropped 25%. Is this risk or opportunity?

## What Marks checks

1. Is this permanent-loss risk or only price volatility?
2. Where are we in the cycle?
3. What is already priced in?

## Decision

Hold for now.

Current evidence points to volatility, not permanent impairment.

## Would be wrong if

1. Balance-sheet or moat damage appears.
2. New evidence shows structural deterioration, not temporary fear.
