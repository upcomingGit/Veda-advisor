# Example 04 - Fisher: Hold a Compounder or Sell

## User question

This company is growing fast. Should I sell after a big run?

## What Fisher checks

1. Are growth drivers still real?
2. Is management still high quality?
3. Has the business weakened on the core Fisher points?

## Decision

Hold.

No evidence of a material quality break. Price rise alone is not a sell reason.

## Would be wrong if

1. Management quality deteriorates.
2. Product runway or research quality clearly weakens.
