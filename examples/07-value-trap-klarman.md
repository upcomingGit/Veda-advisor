# Example 07 - Klarman: Cheap Stock or Value Trap

## User question

This stock looks cheap on P/E. Should I buy?

## What Klarman checks

1. Why is it cheap?
2. Is there a trap risk (secular decline, weak balance sheet, bad governance)?
3. Is margin of safety large enough?

## Decision

Wait.

Price is low, but trap risk is not yet ruled out.

## Would be wrong if

1. New evidence clears the trap concerns.
2. Margin of safety widens to the required threshold.
