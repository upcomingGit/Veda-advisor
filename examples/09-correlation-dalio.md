# Example 09 - Dalio: Diversified on Paper, Concentrated in Reality

## User question

I hold 10 stocks. Am I diversified?

## What Dalio checks

1. Real factor exposure, not just position count.
2. Correlation across positions.
3. Regime holes in the portfolio.

## Decision

Not diversified enough.

Too many positions map to the same factor exposure.

## Would be wrong if

1. Correlation data shows these positions are truly independent.
2. The user intentionally wants concentrated factor exposure and accepts that risk.
