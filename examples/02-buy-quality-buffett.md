# Example 02 - Buffett: Buy or Wait on a Quality Business

## User question

Should I buy this high-quality consumer company now, or wait for a better price?

## What Buffett checks

1. Circle of competence: Do I understand how it makes money?
2. Moat: Is the advantage durable?
3. Margin of safety: Is price below a conservative value range?

## Decision

Wait.

Business quality looks strong, but the margin of safety is too thin for this profile.

## Would be wrong if

1. New data raises conservative intrinsic value enough to restore margin of safety.
2. The current price falls into the target buy zone.
